KAINE MURADINA - Garin Kaduna - Wattpad
Misalin Karfe Hudu da rabi Habeeb da Kannensa Ihsan da Nusaiba ne a zaune Suna fira, yana tambaya gameda karatunsu
Nusaiba dai tana ajin karshe ne a babbar makarantar sakandare tana S
S III
ita tana karatu ne a makarantar gwannati, Su kuwa Ihsan da Affan suna karatu a makarantu masu zaman kansu
Affan ne karaminsu ita kuwa Nusaiba itace ke biwa Habeeb
Baifi tsawon dakiku goma sha biyar da fara firan nasu ba sai sukaji alamar ana sallama a cikin gidan Ihsan itace ta tashi domin ganin wanda yake sallamar
Tana fitowa sukai arba da Zahra'uh
Ruguwa tayi a guje tare ambaton "Oyoyo anty, Oyoyo
", suka rugume juna cikin farin ciki
Ina fatan ummah na ciki ko?, Ihsan tace ah'ah ta dan shiga makauta amma yanzu tana hanyar dawowa, Amma yaya Habeeb yana ciki
Yaushe Ya habeeb din yazo?, inji Zahara
Sannan Ihsan tace about Two weeks
Jim kadan Ihsan ta dauki kayan anty Zahra'uh suka shiga ciki
Kowa yayi murna da zuwan Zahra'uh, musamman Ummah data dawo
Washe gari Nusaiba ta hada masu tea sukai karin kumallo, sannan ta sanya uniform don zuwa makaranta, umma ce ta bata kudin taxi domin a kwai nisa makarantar
Ita kuwa Ihsan zumudin anty zahra'uh ya hanata shakat akan gudanar da aiki kamar yadda ta saba, don haka ko shirin zuwa school batayi ba
Akwai shakuwa mai yawa tsakanin Zahra'uh da Ihsan fiyeda kowa
Ko shima Habeeb dole yabasu fili a yanzu
Duk da cewa Habeeb da Zahra'uh sun shaku matuka, amma yanzu sai Ihsan ta soma mamaye zuciyar zahra
Soyayyar Habeeb tana kokarin shammatar zuciyar zahra'uh, amma taki bayyana hakan
Habeeb Ya cika mako ukku a gida kuma yanason komawa makarantar su mako mai zuwa
